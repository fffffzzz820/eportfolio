About Me

Hello! I am Fan Zhang, a second-year Ph.D. student in Educational Technology at the University of Florida. Originally from China, I am passionate about exploring the applications of AI in Education. My research focuses on enhancing teaching and learning experiences using cutting-edge technologies, with a particular emphasis on the role of large language models (LLMs) in reducing cognitive load for K-12 students.
