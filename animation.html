Animation: Understanding Addition in Math

I explore the concept of addition in math using a fun and interactive example!

In this short animation, I’ve created a scenario with an apple tree to demonstrate how addition works. First, we see one apple falling from the tree. A moment later, two more apples fall. The question is: how many apples have fallen in total? To find out, we use the "+" symbol, which represents combining numbers to calculate a total. By counting the apples, we can see that 1 + 2 equals 3.

Through this animation, I’ve incorporated meaningful visuals, motion tweening, and smooth transitions to make the learning process engaging and easy to understand. The goal is to show how math concepts can be brought to life through creative digital tools. I hope you enjoy it!
